DISEC

Language: English • Type: General Assembly

The Disarmament and International Security Committee addresses threats to peace, arms control, and international security cooperation.

Delegates represent member states to debate strategic risks and produce balanced resolutions through diplomacy.

Possible Agendas

  • Regulating autonomous weapons systems and military AI
  • Preventing proliferation of weapons of mass destruction
  • Combating illicit arms trafficking in fragile regions

Final agenda and study guide will be announced soon.
