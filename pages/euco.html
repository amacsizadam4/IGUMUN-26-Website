The European Council

Language: English • Type: Crisis / Regional Body

The European Council brings together heads of state and government to set political direction on urgent regional priorities.

Delegates will engage in high-level negotiation, consensus building, and rapid policy response on complex crises.

Possible Agendas

  • EU energy security and transition under geopolitical pressure
  • Managing migration and asylum policy with burden-sharing mechanisms
  • Coordinated response to hybrid threats and cyberattacks

Final agenda and study guide will be announced soon.
