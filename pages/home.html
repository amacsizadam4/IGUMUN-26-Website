What is MUN?

Model United Nations (MUN) is an academic simulation of the United Nations where students take on the roles of country representatives and debate real-world global issues.

Participants research their assigned countries, deliver speeches, negotiate with other delegates, and draft resolutions collaboratively.

MUN develops public speaking, diplomacy, critical thinking, and leadership skills while fostering global awareness.
