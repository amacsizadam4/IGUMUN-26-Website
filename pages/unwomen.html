UN Women

Language: English • Type: Specialized Committee

UN Women focuses on global gender equality, women empowerment, and inclusive policy development.

Delegates will negotiate on current challenges affecting women and girls and draft practical, collaborative resolutions.

Possible Agendas

  • Addressing gender-based violence in conflict and post-conflict regions
  • Closing the gender gap in digital access, AI, and STEM education
  • Strengthening economic participation and equal pay policies for women

Final agenda and study guide will be announced soon.
